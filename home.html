About Pelanthia

Welcome to Pelanthia, where luxury meets nature. For over a decade, we've been crafting exceptional floral arrangements that transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.

Our passionate team of florists combines artistry with expertise to create bespoke designs tailored to your unique vision. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we bring elegance and sophistication to every occasion.

We source only the finest, freshest blooms from premium growers worldwide, ensuring each arrangement radiates beauty and lasts longer. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us the trusted choice for discerning clients who appreciate the finer things in life.
